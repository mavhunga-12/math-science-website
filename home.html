📚 Math & Physics Notes

Algebra

  • Linear Equations: ax + b = 0
  • Quadratic Equations: ax² + bx + c = 0
  • Substitution: Solve one equation, substitute into the other

Geometry

  • Triangles: Area = ½ × base × height
  • Circles: Area = πr², Circumference = 2πr
  • Pythagorean Theorem: a² + b² = c²

Physics

  • Newton’s First Law: An object stays at rest or moves at constant speed unless acted on by a force
  • Newton’s Second Law: F = ma (Force = mass × acceleration)
  • Newton’s Third Law: For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction
  • Kinetic Energy: KE = ½mv²
  • Momentum: p = mv (momentum = mass × velocity)
  • Speed of Light: ~299,792 km/s
